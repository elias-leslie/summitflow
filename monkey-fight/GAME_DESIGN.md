# Monkey Fight! - Game Design Document

A side-scrolling adventure game for mobile and desktop browsers.

## Story

Coco (young gorilla) and Kiki (capuchin monkey) are best friends living in the jungle. Gnash, an old mandrill bully, constantly steals their bananas and picks on them. Together, they must navigate obstacle courses, collect bananas, and stand up to Gnash.

## Characters

### Coco (Player 1)
- Young male gorilla
- Brave and energetic
- **Special Ability:** Super Speed burst

### Kiki (Player 2 / Co-op)
- Female capuchin monkey
- Clever and agile
- **Special Ability:** Camouflage when perfectly still (Gnash can't see her)

### Gnash (Antagonist)
- Old, scary-looking mandrill
- Bully who steals bananas
- **Special Ability:** Super Strength + Rage mode

## Core Gameplay

- Side-scrolling platformer with obstacle courses
- Collect bananas for level-ups and upgrades
- 2-player local co-op (Coco + Kiki team up)
- Boss encounters with Gnash

## Key Moments

### Game Start
When player clicks "Play", dramatic splash screen:
> **"MONKEY FIGHT!"**

Similar to classic arcade fighting games announcing "Fight!"

## Special Features

### AI-Generated Content
- **Levels:** Procedurally generated obstacle courses
- **Story:** AI-generated cutscenes between levels to keep things fresh

### Mini-Game: Jungle Tag
- First-person perspective switch
- Coco and Kiki play tag / hide-and-seek
- Fun break between main levels

## Platforms

- Mobile browsers (touch controls)
- Desktop browsers (keyboard)
- Hosted at test.summitflow.dev / testapi.summitflow.dev

## Banana Economy

Bananas are the core progression currency:
- Collect during levels
- Spend on upgrades and unlocks
- Level-up thresholds for new abilities

## Tone

- Colorful and fun
- Kid-friendly but challenging
- Satisfying progression
